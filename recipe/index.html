Banana Bread

Ingredients

3 ripe bananas
1/2 cup canola oil
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Grease loaf pan
Mix oil sugar vanilla eggs
Combine flour and baking soda
Mash bananas with fork
Mix together
Bake for 55 minutes
