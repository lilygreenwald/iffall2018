Banana Bread

Ingredients

3 ripe bananas
1/2 cup canola oil
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Grease loaf pan
3. Mix oil sugar vanilla eggs
4. Combine flour and baking soda
5. Mash bananas with fork
6. Mix together
7. Bake for 55 minutes
